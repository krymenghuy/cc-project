CCC Meeting

  1. The CCC shall establish a Secretariat headed by a Secretariat Manager. The Secretariat Manager and the CCC Secretariat shall execute the day-to-day functions as delegated by the CCC.
  2. The CCC Secretariat Manager and staff attend meetings of the CCC but are not voting members.
  3. The CCC Secretariat will support the CCC by performing the following core functions:
  • Support in the development of Concept Notes and Funding Requests: Proactively facilitate and coordinate development of concept notes and funding requests to the Global Fund with the CCC and the Concept note development Task Force
  • Support in Principal Recipient Nomination: Proactively support the identification and nomination of PR(s) for implementation of Global Fund grants
  • Support in Oversight Committee in the oversight of PRs and program implementation:
  1. Manage oversight visits and meetings, including minute taking and reports to CCC
  2. Pro-actively support building the capacity of the OC
  3. Proactively follow up with PRs, including reviewing, interpreting and summarizing PR dashboards and grant data for OC and CCC
